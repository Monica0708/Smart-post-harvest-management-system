Core Features

Everything You Need to Save More, Earn More

From farm to fork — our integrated platform monitors, predicts, and connects every step of your harvest journey.

Real-time IoT Monitoring

Temperature, humidity, ethylene and moisture sensors stream live data every 5 minutes. Get instant alerts before conditions go out of range.

AI Spoilage Prediction

Our ML engine analyzes sensor patterns to predict spoilage 48–72 hours in advance — giving you time to act before losses occur.

Smart Inventory Management

Track stock levels, batch IDs, quality grades and movement history digitally. Auto-generate QR codes for every batch.

Cold Chain Tracking

End-to-end GPS supply chain visibility with sensor-tagged vehicles. Know temperature status at every transit point.

SMS & WhatsApp Alerts

Push notifications, SMS and WhatsApp alerts in your local language. Works on any basic phone — no smartphone required.

Market Linkage

Connect to verified buyers, traders and exporters. Compare live prices across mandis and negotiate better deals in real time.

Analytics Dashboard

Visual reports on losses, revenue, quality trends and predictions. Export data for government submissions or bank loan applications.

Live Market Prices

Real-time commodity prices by state and city from AGMARKNET and e-NAM — refreshed every 30 minutes automatically.

❌ Why Losses Happen

No Temperature Monitoring
Farmers don't know when storage conditions become dangerous for their produce
No Market Price Visibility
Farmers sell at wrong time and wrong place, getting 30–40% less than optimal price
Broken Cold Chain
Temperature spikes during transit destroy produce quality before reaching markets
No Digital Records
Paper-based tracking leads to errors and inability to access bank loans or insurance
